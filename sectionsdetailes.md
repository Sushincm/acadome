Homepage — Section Order
Keep every section below in this exact order. Do not remove or reorder.
1. Nav
Sticky top nav. Logo left. Links: Home · About Us · Our Courses · Gallery · Contact Us. CTA button right: "Enroll Now →" in navy fill. On scroll past hero — nav gets a white background with a subtle shadow. Mobile: hamburger menu, full-screen overlay nav.
2. Hero
Dark navy background. Large Sora heading — bold, 2 lines max. Subtext in muted white, 1–2 lines. Two CTA buttons: primary "Explore Courses →" (red fill) and ghost "Learn More" (white outline). Right side: a strong hero image or an abstract geometric visual representing finance/accounting. Scroll-down arrow indicator at the bottom center.
3. About Us
White background. Two-column layout — left: section label + heading + 2–3 paragraph description of Acadome's mission. Right: a photo of the institute or classroom. Below the text: 3 small stat chips in a row — e.g. "10+ Years Experience · 500+ Students · GCC Certified." Keep it factual, confirm numbers with client.

Dark navy background. 3-card grid — Internship · Workshop · Seminar Sessions. Each card: icon, title, short description, "Read More →" link. Cards have semi-transparent white surface on the dark bg (rgba(255,255,255,0.05) fill, 1px rgba(255,255,255,0.1) border). This section is mandatory on the homepage.

Dark navy background (continues from What We Offer). 3 cards side by side — RSDCA Certification · Embassy Attestation · SAP Authorised Certification. Each card: small badge top-right (National / International / Global), logo or icon circle, issuer label in red uppercase, title, short description. Scroll reveal stagger 0ms · 120ms · 240ms.

White background. Tab or numbered-index switcher — 3 courses: CCAP (2 months + internship) · DAFA (6 months + internship) · SAP FICO (3 months + internship). Each tab shows: left side — duration pill, course name, description, topic list, "Enroll Now →" button. Right side — course image with a floating duration badge. 7. Key Features
Off-white background. 4-card grid — Hands-on Training · Industry-Relevant Curriculum · Experienced Instructors · Flexible Learning Options. Each card: navy icon box (40×40px, border-radius 10px, white icon), bold title, short description.
8. Software Tools Marquee
White background. Section label "Cloud-Based Accounting Softwares." A continuous auto-scrolling marquee row of software logos — Zoho · Xero · QuickBooks · Sage · Odoo · Tally. No pause on hover. Logos in grayscale, full color on hover. Speed: slow and smooth.

Dark navy background. Section heading "Our Mentors: Elevating Your Path to Excellence." Team cards in a horizontal scroll carousel (drag-to-scroll with progress bar below). Each card: photo, name, qualification (CA / MBA etc), designation "Faculty." On desktop show 3–4 cards. On mobile: card width 75vw, scroll snap, progress bar fills as user scrolls.

White background. Top: section label + heading "We Don't Stop at Training" + subtext. Global Rules Across All Pages
Navigation is identical on all pages — sticky, same links, same CTA button. Active page link gets a red underline or slightly bolder weight.
Footer is identical on all pages.
All pages use IntersectionObserver scroll reveal — opacity 0 translateY(20px) → opacity 1 translateY(0), 550ms ease-out, 80ms stagger for multi-element rows.
Breakpoint: 768px. All multi-column grids collapse to single column. Section padding reduces from 80px vertical to 48px vertical. Horizontal padding from 7vw to 5vw.
All CTA buttons use the "→" arrow suffix — consistent across all pages.
WhatsApp float button appears on every page — fixed position bottom-right, navy background, white WhatsApp icon, 52×52px circle, slight bounce animation on load.
Image lazy loading — add loading="lazy" to every image that is below the fold.
